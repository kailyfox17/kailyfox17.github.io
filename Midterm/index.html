Introduction: Who is Kaily Fox?

I am Kaily Fox, a current Senior at the University of Colorado Boulder. I am a finance major in the Leeds School of Business, along with a minor in Creative Design & Technology accompied with a certificate in Global Business.

I have loved living in Boulder, Colorado the last four years. My time here has been filled with amazing adventures and opportunities that I am eternally grateful for. I will cheerish the friends met and the memories I have made for a lifetime.

Throughout these last four years, I have become even a bigger fan of this state than I previously was. I love being outdoors, and Colorado provides almost no excuse to not. Boulder is filled with beautiful hiking spots and scenery that take my breath away each day. I also grew up skiing, so being able to have such easy access to some of the best resorts in the country is the biggest blessing.

Now that my time in this amazing place has come to an end, it is time for me to start looking forward. I am orginally from a western suburb of Chicago and I plan to move back to the city after graduation. Although I do not know exactly what my next steps are, I am eager to start my career as a women in finance. I feel ready to be independent and make Chicago my own.

I hope to combine my passions and skills to make a meaningful and successful career for myself. I hold myself to a high standard and I take pride in my work ethics and aspiration. With my personal qualities and all the valuable skills I have learned at CU, I feel ready for the next chapter of my life.
